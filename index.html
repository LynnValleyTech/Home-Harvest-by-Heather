Heather's Garden Philosophy

With over 15 years of experience creating beautiful outdoor spaces in Vancouver, Heather combines horticultural expertise with artistic vision. Certified in sustainable landscape practices, she specializes in designs that thrive in our unique Pacific Northwest climate while respecting local ecosystems.

Heather believes gardens should be:

  • Ecologically sound
  • Beautiful in all seasons
  • Functional for your lifestyle
  • Filled with sensory delights
